# Q4 2024 Business Review

**Date:** January 6, 2025
**Attendees:** Sarah Mitchell (CEO), David Park (VP Sales), Emma Rodriguez (VP Marketing), Tom Bradley (CFO), Rachel Kim (VP Product)

## Executive Summary

Q4 exceeded revenue targets by 12%. Customer acquisition costs decreased but churn increased in the SMB segment.

## Key Decisions

1. Sarah approved the expansion into the European market, targeting a Q2 2025 launch in UK and Germany.
2. The leadership team decided to increase the enterprise sales team from 8 to 15 reps by end of Q1.
3. Tom recommended and the team agreed to raise Series B at $50M valuation in March rather than waiting until June.
4. We will sunset the Basic tier and migrate all users to Pro by April 1st.

## Commitments from Leadership

- **David** committed to closing 3 enterprise deals worth $500K+ ARR by end of Q1.
- **Emma** will launch the new brand positioning campaign by February 15th with a $2M budget.
- **Rachel** will deliver the mobile app MVP by March 30th.
- **Tom** will have the Series B pitch deck and financials ready for investor meetings by February 1st.

### Marketing (Emma)
